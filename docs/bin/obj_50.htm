domapi.resize

Resize uses selection to place controls on an elm and allow the user to resize the elm. Resize can be turned on for an individual elm or for a group of elms.

 

 

Methods unique to domapi.resize

  • off
  • on
Methods
off ()

turns off resize for a group of elms

back to top
on ()

turns on resize for a group of elms
